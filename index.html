About Us

Panchamrit Dairy Udhyog was established in the year …. by Chandra Bahadur Rai with the objective of uplifting economic growth of the poor farming communities along with the motive of providing qualitative and hygienic milk products in the Nepalese market by delivering the products that are made in Suyrodaya Municipality (Ilam).As Ilam is regarded one of the finest and purest milk production place in Nepal. Certainly inaugurating such industry would be the boost from various perspectives in terms of economic growth & upliftment in the aspects of providing employment opportunities for the general people of the community through the proper consumption of milk that is produced in the community.

Our Objective

Provide a guaranteed market for milk to the rural farmers with fair price.
Supply pasteurized milk and milk products to urban consumers.
Develop organized milk collection system to meet increasing demand for pasteurized milk and milk products.
Develop an organized marketing system for milk and milk products in urban areas.
Supply qualitative and hygienic milk produts for the general people in city areas.
